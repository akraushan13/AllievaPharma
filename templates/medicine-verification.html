How to Validate a Genuine Product


Scratch security label on the product to Unhide medicine authentication code.

Enter your country , contact number , medicine authentication code & Write the answer to a mathematical question. Or captcha

Press the Verify tab. On successful validation " Congratulations your Product is found Genuine " message would be displayed.

.In order to provide more precise verification we have begin the process to provide the Product name , batch , manufacturing & expiry dates as well on successful verification. However, products in the market before this additional feature will display " Congratulations your Product is found Genuine " message but may not display product data. In that case too Product should be deemed Genuine.

In case your Product fails the Authentication " Product Verification failed" messege would be displayed .
